FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. What is the purpose of this platform?

Answer: Our platform aims to bridge the gap between the government and the public, providing a channel for reporting and resolving community issues, such as potholes, safety concerns, or infrastructure problems.

2. Is this platform accessible on mobile devices?

Answer: Yes, our platform is mobile-responsive, allowing you to report issues or check on the status of reported problems from your smartphone or tablet.

3. What do I do if an issue is not being addressed satisfactorily?

Answer: If you are not satisfied with the resolution of an issue, you can provide feedback through the platform. We encourage you to share your concerns and suggestions for improvement.
